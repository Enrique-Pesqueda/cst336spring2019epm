What does Grey Hat Hacker mean?

There are generally three different types of hackers. Grey Hat, White Hat, and Black Hat Hackers. White hat hackers work to make systems stronger, but grey hat hackers find vulnerabilities and report them to their owners. However sometimes these independent hackers ask for a fee to improve their security. If there is no compliance, grey hat hackers often post the exploit online.
