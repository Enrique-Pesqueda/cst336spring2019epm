What is Hacking?

Hacking is the intent to to find weaknesses in systems or networks to gain control
or information. An example of hacking is using a password cracking algorithm.
Isolating your systems is not enough to secure networks because you need to communicate
with the outside world in order to send and receive information. Hacking itself is to
commit fraudulent crimes such as invading private info. Today Hacking has cost many
organizations millions of dollars.
