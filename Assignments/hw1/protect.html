What does Black Hat Hacker mean?

A black hat hacker strictly does malicious acts. Their goal is find and exploit any vulnerabilities a system may have. Black hat hackers can inflict damage on both private and large corporations, and have even been able to shut down systems ending up in millions of dollars lost.
